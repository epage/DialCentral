About

DialCentral (formerly called GC Dialer) is meant as an interface to Google's GoogleVoice service that integrates with your Maemo device or Linux computer.

DialCentral has been tested on Maemo 5, Maemo 4.1, Ubuntu 9.04, and Mer 0.16-testing5. Go check out some reviews (just don't use that old download link) or join the conversation

DialCentral is Free Software and available under the LGPLv2.1.

Features

DialCentral v1.0.8-5 (See Maemo.Org Talk)

  • Dialpad interface to click-to-call
  • Ability to send SMS messages through GoogleVoice
  • Recent calls list for easily calling people back
  • History of voicemail transcripts and SMS conversations
  • Contacts list, pulling from GoogleVoice and CSV Files (like GrandCentral and GoogleContact exports)
  • Recent Call history, voicemail, and SMS notification (Maemo 4.1 only)
  • Shortcuts:
    • Hold down any tab to force it to refresh
    • Dialpad: hold down the back button to change it to clear
    • Ctrl+c from SMS Dialog to copy message history
    • Ctrl+v on dialpad to paste a number
    • Ctrl+l to copy the debug log
    • Ctrl+enter to toggle fullscreen
